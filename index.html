UWAZI Foundation

Is a decentralized charity foundation aimed at promoting trust and transparency in the world of donations. Our CO-Pillars are:-

  • ZERO Hunger
  • Life on land
  • Clean water and sanitation
  • Youth and Children Development

UWAZI Tokens

UWAZI foundation will have a utility tokens (uwazI) that will have real intrisic value after the ICO phase. The tokens will be:-

  • Used as a mode of payment.
  • Used to acces uwazi charity foundation services.
  • Used to run operations on the Uwazi FOundation ECosystem.
  • Reward holders(POS) reward block producers(POW).

TOKEN SALE

Premined UF(UWAZI Foundation) tokens will be sold to members of the public. Funds raised during the crowd offring will be used to steer UF operations and set payments such as:-

  • Fund UF(UWAZI FOUNDATION) ecosystem CO-Pillars.
  • Fund uwazi foundation project Development
  • Market and advertising
  • Payment of human resource
  • property acquisition
  • listing on exchanges
